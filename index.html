WHO ARE WE???


Cr8ech solutions and projects(Pty) Ltd is a registered website and graphics corporation that offers small businesses the opportunity to expand their business online and to reach more people, this hereby increase business productivity and may boost sales.

The company was started in the year 2016, which was first an idea between two determined, motivated and young individuals, who sat down to put their ideas on paper. Then after they went on to register the company Cr8ech solution and projects.

Over the times we have been questioned about how the name 'Cr8ech' came about and the meaning of the name. Well, the name Cr8ech is a unique combination of two word which are create and tech. the '8' in cr8ech is what bonds the two words. The '8' takes the place of the letters 'eate' in 'create' and the 't' in 'tech'.
